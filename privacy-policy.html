Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026  ·  Hemphill Counseling Services, PLLC

This Privacy Policy describes how Hemphill Counseling Services, PLLC ("HCS," "we," "us") collects and handles information submitted through this website. It applies to this public website only — not to clinical records or protected health information, which are governed separately by our Notice of Privacy Practices provided at intake.

What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to hemphillcounselingservices.com and any information you submit through it — such as sending an email inquiry using the contact address listed on this site. It does not apply to the SimplePractice or Headway client portals, which are governed by their own privacy policies and by HIPAA.

Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily. If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include in that message — typically your name, email address, and the content of your inquiry. We do not operate a contact form on this website. All direct contact occurs via email or through the Headway or SimplePractice booking portals.

Information collected automatically. This website does not currently use tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics. Standard server logs may record basic technical information such as the date and time of a request and the type of browser used. This information is not linked to any individual and is not used for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted via email is used solely to respond to your inquiry and, if you proceed to schedule services, to facilitate your care. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Clinical Records and HIPAA

This website is not a clinical platform. If you are an existing or prospective client, your protected health information (PHI) is handled in accordance with our Notice of Privacy Practices, which is provided separately through your client portal at intake. For questions about your clinical records or privacy rights under HIPAA, please contact us directly at [email protected].

Third-Party Services

This website links to Headway and SimplePractice for booking. Once you leave this site and interact with those platforms, their respective privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review them before submitting personal information.

This site uses Google Fonts, which may result in a request to Google's servers when the page loads. No personally identifiable information is transmitted in this process.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your browser may store basic session data as part of normal web operation, but no cookies are placed by HCS for analytics, retargeting, or marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18 through this website. HCS does not provide therapy services to minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of this website after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
